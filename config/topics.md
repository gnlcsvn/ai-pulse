# Topics & Search Configuration

Edit this file to control what the pipeline searches for and who it prioritizes.

## Search Queries
Used as YouTube search terms. `{year}` is replaced with the current year.

- AI interview {year}
- artificial intelligence CEO interview
- AI leader interview podcast
- AI company founder interview
- frontier AI models discussion
- AGI interview {year}
- AI safety interview
- machine learning research interview
- LLM breakthrough interview
- AI startup founder interview {year}

## Keywords Include
Video titles must contain at least one of these to be considered.

interview, podcast, fireside chat, conversation with, talks to, speaks with, in conversation, Q&A, keynote, panel discussion

## Keywords Exclude
Videos with these in the title are skipped.

tutorial, how to, course, learn, beginner, top 10, best tools, free AI tools, demo
