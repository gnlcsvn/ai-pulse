# YouTube Channels

Curated list of channels to scan for AI interviews. Edit freely - the pipeline reads this file on each run.

## High Priority
Channels that consistently produce high-quality AI interviews. Always scan these.

### Dwarkesh Patel
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@DwarkeshPatel/videos
- Type: Deep-dive AI interviews
- Best long-form AI interviews. Dario Amodei, Elon Musk, Ilya Sutskever.

### Lex Fridman
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@lexfridman/videos
- Type: Long-form interviews
- Major AI figures, very long episodes (2-4h). Filter for AI-relevant ones.

### No Priors
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@NoPriorsPodcast/videos
- Type: AI-focused podcast
- Sarah Guo & Elad Gil. Short, focused AI industry episodes (~40m).

### Lenny's Podcast
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@LennysPodcast/videos
- Type: Product/tech interviews
- Product-focused but increasingly AI-heavy. Boris Cherny, OpenAI engineers.

### 20VC with Harry Stebbings
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@20VC/videos
- Type: VC/startup interviews
- Frequent AI CEO interviews. Codex lead, Klarna CEO, ElevenLabs.

## Medium Priority
Good channels but need filtering - not every episode is AI-relevant.

### a16z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@a16z/videos
- Type: Tech/AI discussions
- VC perspective on AI markets. Filter for AI-specific episodes.

### Financial Times
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@FinancialTimes/videos
- Type: News interviews
- Occasional high-value AI interviews (Mustafa Suleyman, Demis Hassabis). Look for "FT Interview" tag.

### 80,000 Hours
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@eaborightytwothousandhours/videos
- Type: Long-form AI safety/impact
- AI safety and existential risk focus. Very long episodes.

### The Diary Of A CEO
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@TheDiaryOfACEO/videos
- Type: CEO interviews
- Steven Bartlett. Occasional AI leaders (Geoffrey Hinton, etc). Filter for AI topics.

### All-In Podcast
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@allin/videos
- Type: Tech/business podcast
- Chamath, Sacks, Friedberg, Calacanis. Covers AI market trends weekly.

### The Logan Bartlett Show
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@LoganBartlett/videos
- Type: Tech interviews
- VC/tech interviews. Sporadic posting.

## Low Priority
Scan occasionally or only when specific guests are featured.

### NVIDIA
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@NVIDIA/videos
- Type: Company channel
- Jensen Huang keynotes. Mostly product content - filter for interviews/keynotes only.

### TED
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@TED/videos
- Type: Talks
- Occasional AI talks (Yoshua Bengio, etc). Very high volume - filter carefully.

### World Economic Forum
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@wef/videos
- Type: Policy/industry events
- Davos panels. Elon Musk, AI policy discussions. Seasonal (Jan/Feb).

### Eye on AI
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/@eyeonai/videos
- Type: AI industry interviews
- AI-specific. Check if still actively posting.
